Trend

This graph explores education over 8 years period. The data takes different age group, ranging from 15 years to 85+ years for both male and female. The data takes the highest percentage of year 12 graduates (from differnet age group) from all the location in New South Wales.

Results Observable from the graph, it is clear that there females have a higher amount of graduates initially. However the steep and also steady decline over the new 2 years is quite significant. Both results for males and females are fairly similar in trends where they both drop towards 2008-2009 but eventually begins inclining. This data possibly expresses a successful change in the education system however, although it was effective for many years, the lack of newer change ultimately caused the incline to drop, even though the incline was increasingly increasing. Some could argue the increase of technology was to be complimented for the increase of graduates and ultimately the change in polarity of increase/ decrease.
